Outward 2 players participating in the early playtests are reporting an issue when trying to specialize their characters at the Mastat Cultist. According to community reports, players find themselves unable to acquire specific high-level skills, such as Bloodlust, even when they appear to meet all the necessary prerequisites.

The issue, first highlighted on the game's subreddit, details a situation where a player attempted to purchase the Bloodlust specialization. Despite carrying the required 300 silver and studying the prerequisite books, the "acquire" option remains grayed out in the trainer interface.

### A Bug or a Hidden Requirement?

At this stage of the Outward 2 beta, it is unclear whether this is a direct bug or if there is an unconfirmed mechanic at play. In the original Outward, skill trainers often had strict progression paths, and some specializations were mutually exclusive. The community speculation suggests a few possibilities for the grayed-out button:

* **Faction or Alignment Lock:** The Mastat Cultist might require a specific faction alignment or a minimum reputation level that isn't clearly indicated in the user interface.
* **Stats Threshold:** Bloodlust or other cultist skills might require a minimum threshold in mana, health, or specific attributes before they can be purchased.
* **Prerequisite Skills:** While the player reported learning all the required books, they might be missing a basic skill from the same tree that acts as an invisible prerequisite.
* **An Active Bug:** Since the game is still in development, this could simply be a UI bug or a database error preventing the trainer interaction from registering properly.

### Troubleshooting Steps for the Outward 2 Beta

If you run into this trainer issue during your playthrough, here are a few basic Outward 2 tips to try before abandoning the build:

1. **Relog and Reload:** Save your game, exit to the main menu, and reload. Sometimes trainer interfaces fail to update your inventory status (like silver count or read books) until the session is refreshed.
2. **Check Your Books:** Double-check that the books you read actually match the exact prerequisites for Bloodlust. In complex RPGs, similar-sounding items can easily be confused.
3. **Submit a Bug Report:** If you believe you have met every requirement and the option remains locked, use the developer's official feedback channels to report the issue. Providing your save file and specifying the Mastat Cultist can help the team patch it in future updates.

We will update this Outward 2 guide as more players test the Mastat Cultist specialization and confirm whether this is a mechanical restriction or a bug.
